# 📊 Business Insights & Recommendations

## 📌 Executive Summary

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using **Python, PostgreSQL, SQL, and Power BI** to uncover purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and revenue trends. The analysis transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this analysis were to:

- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify high-performing product categories.
- Measure the effectiveness of discount strategies.
- Analyze subscriber vs. non-subscriber spending.
- Evaluate customer loyalty and repeat purchases.
- Discover revenue trends across demographics.
- Generate actionable business recommendations.

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights

## 👕 1. Product Category Performance

### Observation

- Clothing generated the highest overall revenue.
- Accessories and Footwear were also strong-performing categories.
- Outerwear contributed comparatively lower revenue.

### Business Impact

High-performing categories drive the majority of business revenue.

### Recommendation

- Increase inventory for top-selling categories.
- Promote low-performing categories using targeted campaigns.
- Cross-sell accessories with clothing purchases.

---

## 👥 2. Customer Demographics

### Observation

- Young Adult customers contributed the highest revenue.
- Senior customers accounted for a smaller share of total sales.

### Business Impact

Marketing efforts are most effective when focused on high-value age groups.

### Recommendation

- Design personalized campaigns for Young Adults.
- Create special offers for Senior customers to increase engagement.

---

## 💳 3. Subscription Analysis

### Observation

- Subscribers spent more on average than non-subscribers.
- Subscription programs positively influenced purchasing behavior.

### Business Impact

Subscription membership increases customer lifetime value.

### Recommendation

- Expand subscription benefits.
- Encourage non-subscribers to join through exclusive rewards.

---

## 🏷️ 4. Discount Effectiveness

### Observation

- Many customers who received discounts still spent above the average purchase amount.
- Discounts encouraged higher-value purchases instead of reducing revenue.

### Business Impact

Well-targeted discounts improve sales without significantly affecting profitability.

### Recommendation

- Continue targeted promotional campaigns.
- Offer discounts based on customer behavior instead of applying them universally.

---

## 🚚 5. Shipping Preferences

### Observation

- Customers selecting Express Shipping generally had a higher average purchase amount.

### Business Impact

Premium shipping attracts higher-value customers.

### Recommendation

- Bundle Express Shipping with premium membership plans.
- Offer free Express Shipping above a spending threshold.

---

## ⭐ 6. Product Ratings

### Observation

- Highly rated products consistently generated stronger sales.
- Customer satisfaction positively influenced purchasing decisions.

### Business Impact

Customer reviews directly affect product performance.

### Recommendation

- Highlight highly rated products.
- Improve quality of lower-rated products.

---

## 🔁 7. Customer Loyalty

### Observation

- Repeat customers generated a significant portion of revenue.
- Loyal customers purchased more frequently.

### Business Impact

Customer retention is more cost-effective than acquiring new customers.

### Recommendation

- Launch loyalty reward programs.
- Provide personalized offers to repeat buyers.
